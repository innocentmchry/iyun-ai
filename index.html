About इयुन AI

Iyun AI is designed to assist with multilingual conversations, providing translation and intelligent responses in both Bodo and English. The name "इयुन" or “Iyun” means “Future” in Bodo language, reflecting our vision for the cultural future of the Bodo language and its rich heritage. By leveraging advanced natural language processing, Iyun AI delivers accurate, context-aware answers. As part of our commitment to linguistic empowerment, data collected through Iyun AI will be used to train an independent, community-driven large language models or to fine-tune state-of-the-art models, ensuring that Bodo language and culture continue to thrive in the digital age.
